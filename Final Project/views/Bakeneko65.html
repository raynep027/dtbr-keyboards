Bakeneko 65

An always in-stock board at Cannon Keys, the Bakeneko 65 is the bigger brother to the original Bakeneko 60. This keyboard comes with 14 different colour options and a removable silicone weight. It comes with a travel case so you can conveniently type in style anywhere you go.

Barebones Kit

  • Layout: 65%
  • Colour: Black
  • Weight: 590g (Unbuilt)
  • Case Material: CNC Aluminum
  • QMK/VIA Compatible
  • Hot-Swappable
  • South-Facing RGB
  • O-Ring Gasket Mount
  • FR4 Universal Layout Plate

Switches

  • Manufacturer: Haimu
  • Top Housing Material: Nylon PA66
  • Bottom Housing Material: Nylon PA66
  • Stem Material: POM

The Haimu Heartbeat Silent switches are a great option for anyone trying to be considerate of the other typists around them, offering a quiet and still satisfying typing experience.
